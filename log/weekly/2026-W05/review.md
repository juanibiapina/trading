## Key Lessons Confirmed

### 1. Stop losses don't work in extended hours
- PZG had SL at $2.49, stock crashed to $1.70 - didn't trigger
- Critical risk in AH/PM trading

### 2. Premarket peaks, then dumps at open (Trading Plan insight validated)
- BATL: Peaked $7.00 PM, dumped to $2.55 after open
- MRNO: Peaked ~$4.10 at open, crashed to $2.31 by EOD
- DRCT: TP hit in premarket, gave back most gains at open

### 3. FOMO kills
- PZG: "Bought too fast - felt anxious/FOMO as price was rising quickly"
- Result: Bought at literal top, -50% loss

### 4. Catalyst quality matters
- BATL (operational news): +29% win
- AGMH (financing/dilution): -5.2% loss
- VIVS (partnership): +65.9% win
- SER (FDA IND): Faded despite strong catalyst

## Recommendations

1. **Enforce 1 trade/day rule** - Violated 3 of 4 days. Consider adding friction.

2. **No stop losses in extended hours** - Either accept full loss potential or size positions smaller for AH trades.

3. **Entry discipline** - Avoid buying >50% above previous close in AH. PZG (+97%) and SER (+69%) both failed.

4. **Stick to biotech** - All 3 wins were biotech-adjacent. Non-biotech trades lost.

5. **Exit in premarket, not at open** - Best exits (MRNO, VIVS) were before/at open. Open dumps are real.
