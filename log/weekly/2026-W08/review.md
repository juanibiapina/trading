## Recommendations

1. **Hard filter non-biotech names** — If ticker is not biotech/pharma, skip automatically.
2. **Require catalyst proof before entry** — No verifiable catalyst = no trade, regardless of volume.
3. **Add minimum relative volume rule** — Require rel vol >2x for AH entries.
4. **Set exit workflow immediately after entry** — Alarm + premarket sell order/checklist before sleep.
5. **Prefer no trade over B/C setups** — Wait for A-grade catalyst + sector alignment.
