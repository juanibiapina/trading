# Trading Plan Requirements

Use these questions when defining or updating a trading plan. A complete plan should address each section.

## Strategy

- [ ] What is the core edge? (Why does this strategy work?)
- [ ] What patterns or setups will you trade?
- [ ] What sectors or asset types?
- [ ] What timeframe? (Scalping, day trading, swing, position)

## Schedule

- [ ] When will you screen for candidates?
- [ ] When will you enter trades?
- [ ] When will you exit trades?
- [ ] When will you review performance? (Daily, weekly, monthly)

## Risk Management

- [ ] How much capital per trade? (Fixed amount or % of account)
- [ ] Maximum positions at once?
- [ ] Maximum trades per day/week?
- [ ] Daily/weekly loss limit to stop trading?
- [ ] Will you use stop losses? At what level?
- [ ] Will you use margin? When and how much?

## Trade Execution

- [ ] Entry: All at once or scale in?
- [ ] Exit: All at once or take partial profits?
- [ ] Where will partial profits be taken?
- [ ] What confirms a valid entry? (Checklist)
- [ ] What invalidates a setup? (When to skip)

## Decision Making

- [ ] Will you make real-time decisions or plan the night before?
- [ ] What tools/platforms will you use?
- [ ] How will you avoid emotional decisions?

## Record Keeping

- [ ] How will you log trades?
- [ ] What metrics will you track?
- [ ] How will you identify and learn from mistakes?
